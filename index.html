At CycleBar, borrowing cycling shoes is complimentary, while at SoulCycle, each shoe rental is an additional $4 charge. Moreover, if it's your first time, reaching out to the instructor via direct message may earn you a free guest bike.

Although not as regular as CycleBar, SoulCycle also periodically offers discounts. For my initial visit, I took advantage of a special promotion of 2 weeks of classes for just $50 , and I took classes every day.
